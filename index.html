Half-Hearted Retreat is a quiet, peaceful place right outside of Louisville, in New Albany IN. All artists and writers are welcome to attend during open hours. Time is not structured. People are welcome to come and go at any point during the open hours. Teachers and organizers are welcome to reserve time to run their own workshops and use the space. Internet connection is not available so this is truly an opportunity to unplug, work, and create.


$5 donation will be asked at the door (cash only). Coffee is provided. You are welcome to bring your own food, snacks, or drinks. As always, be mindful and kind to those working around you.
